  1. The first building built in Dnipro is the "Potemkin Palace" in 1790, now the "Students' Palace"

  2. There is no specific date for the City Day in Dnipro. It is celebrated on the second Saturday of September

  3. Dnipro has the largest Embankment in Europe - 23 km

  4. Zenit missiles are manufactured in Dnipro, which still have no analogues in the whole world

  5. The world's largest Jewish centre "Menorah" is located in Dnipro

  6. The oldest and largest collection of "Stone Women" is located in the Dnipro Historical Museum

  7. The first reinforced concrete bridge in the world and the largest arch bridge in Europe "Merefo-Khersonsky" is located in Dnipro

  8. Dnipro metro is the smallest in Europe, 6 stations, line length 7.8 km

  9. The longest residential building in Ukraine is the nine-storey "Chinese Wall" on Heroiv Avenue

  10. The tallest building in Dnipro is the "Towers" at 123 metres
